A complete ground-up restoration of a 1973 Jeep CJ5. Every system has been touched: frame inspection and repair, suspension rebuild, drivetrain overhaul, bodywork, and engine work. 7.3L Powerstroke Diesel Engine Rebuilds

Multiple full rebuilds of Ford's 7.3L Powerstroke diesel — one of the most durable diesel engines ever produced, and unforgiving if the details aren't right. Work includes precision measurement and machining tolerances, injector service, head gasket replacement, turbocharger inspection, and full reassembly to factory spec.
